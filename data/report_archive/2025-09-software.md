Welcome to your SOTD Lather Log for September 2025

* 1,765 shave reports from 135 distinct shavers during the month of September 2025 were analyzed to produce this report. Collectively, these shavers used 590 distinct soaps from 135 distinct brands. 58 of those shaves used samples of 39 unique soaps.

## Observations

* After Austere August’s paltry 181 distinct soaps, September was a comparative smorgasbord with 590 soaps used.

* Summer Break Soaps is having a moment. After just 44 shaves in July (skipping over Austere August becuase Austere), shavers lathered it up 165 times this month placing it third behind the ever-victorious Stirling and Barrister and Mann.

* *Catie’s Bubbles edges past Barrister and Mann to take silver in the Brand Diversity contest.

* Punk-rock soap is in: #7 Fanzine Saponifications landed two scents in the top 10: This Is Not a Test at #4 and Caravaggio at #8.

* “Sample September” was kind of a thing: 58 shaves (3.3%) used trial sizes.

## Notes & Caveats

* Tables show all entries with 5 or more shaves to provide comprehensive coverage without artificial row limits.

* The unique user column shows the number of different users who used a given brand/soap/etc in the month.

* The Brand Diversity table details the number of distinct soaps used during the month from that particular brand, showing all brands with 5+ unique soaps.

* The change Δ vs columns show how an item has moved up or down the rankings since that month. = means no change in position, up or down arrows indicate how many positions up or down the rankings an item has moved compared to that month. n/a means the item was not present in that month.
